Who We Are

Our Story

From a single storefront in Makkah to a multi-division group serving the GCC — this is the journey of Taj Middleeast.

Our Beginnings

Built on Trust,
Grown Through Quality

Taj Middleeast began as a modest food trading company with one clear mission — to bring quality everyday products to the people of the Arabian Peninsula. Our first outlet, Taj Foods Trading, opened its doors in the heart of Makkah Al Mukarama, serving wholesale buyers and neighbourhood consumers with integrity and care.

What started as shelves stocked with staple groceries quickly grew into a trusted supply chain partner for restaurants, institutions, and retailers across the region. Word spread — not through advertising, but through the reliability of our deliveries and the consistency of our products.

Today, Taj Middleeast has expanded into six distinct divisions — Foods, Recycling, Opticals, Packaging, Electronics, and Perfumes — each built on the same foundation that started in that first Makkah storefront: a commitment to quality that our customers can count on.
